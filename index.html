About Rosé Ribbons

Rosé Ribbons is a small jewellery shop making bracelets, necklaces and phone charms. Everything is handmade and designs change often.

This website is run with a parent/guardian for safety and help with orders.

Custom requests

Want specific colours or a name charm? Add it to your message and we’ll reply.

How to buy

  1. Choose a product and click Request.
  2. Send your message (goes to a parent-managed email address).
  3. We confirm price + postage/collection.
  4. Payment is handled by a parent/guardian.
